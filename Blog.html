P5.JS

What is p5.JS?

p5.js is a library that lets you create visual compositions using JavaScript.

It combines art and programming, allowing users to turn their imaginations into reality through code.

You can develop creative projects ranging from simple geometric shapes to complex interactive designs.

Anatolian Women and p5.js

Crochet and lacework by Anatolian women represent a longstanding tradition and art form.

Code and Stitches: Each crochet stitch creates part of a pattern. Similarly, every line of code in p5.js is crucial for the final artwork.

In the digital age, we can see a modern version of this traditional art using technologies like p5.js!

Both processes show how small steps can lead to big results.

Patterns and Structures: Crochet designs feature repeated motifs. Likewise, creating digital art with p5.js involves using specific coding structures.

The floral patterns in crochet can be just as impressive as fractal designs made with p5.js

This blend combines the skill of the past with today's technology, creating beautiful artworks.

Both crochet and coding require patience, attention, and creativity, producing wonderful results.
